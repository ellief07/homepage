My Beautiful Guitar

Epiphone Hummingbird in Ebony


I grew up playing mulitple instruments but none of them compare to how much I love to play the guitar! I got my first guitar in 2022 and it was a basic Yamaha starter kit. The first song I taught myself to play was Cherry Wine by Hozier. I do not recommend this as a beginner song! My fingers were numb for weeks. In 2023, I decided to use my birthday money to buy an Epiphone Hummingbird in the color Ebony. I wanted a black guitar, came across this model online, and fell in love.





The day I brought her home: November 24, 2023. Happy Birthday!
