Fun facts:


  • Created in the 1990s as a more affordable alternative to the Gibson Hummingbird, which was one of Gibson's most expensive acoustic guitars at the time.
  • Small differences in quality, thousands of dollars difference in price!
  • There are 5 different models, each with slight variations.
  • Color variants include Ebony, Natural, Cherry Sunburst, and more.
